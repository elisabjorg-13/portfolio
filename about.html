About me

Hi I'm Elísa Björg

I am a programmer from Iceland. I graduated from my bachelors in software engineering from University of Iceland in June 2023, after taking my last semester abroad in Madrid at Universidad Pontificia Comillas.

During my time studying my degree, it quickly became clear that my interests were more in the creative and artistic side of computer industry . I found myself drawn to front end design and programming, both for websites and mobile applications for companies I worked for during the summer. Furthermore, I gained a lot of interest in data analization and visualisation after implementing a visual solution for a startup last summer. I feel that presenting data based knowledge in a way that anybody understands, regardless of education or background, is one of the key things for the future. During my time in the University of Iceland, I was a Teaching assistand in Database Theory for professor Snorri Agnarsson, where I taught so called "excersize classes" and revised homework.

I have always had a passion for music and through programming i started getting into music production using FL studios, where a few of my songs and mixes are available on my soundcloud. Music production is a good way for me to combine my creative and nerdy side and me and a fellow programmer friend regularly produce together.

Last but not least, I discovered I had a knack for the entrepreneurial side of the software industry. My startup idea called inkUp is a platform that strives to connect tattoo artists and possible clients, with its goal to make the tattoo industry less underground, benifitting both the seller and the buyer. Whilst in University, I got the chance to practice pitching my idea in front of investors and powerful people in Iceland´s tech industry. Whilst I got good feedback on the idea, what they told me was even better was my ability to stand in front of many people and present my ideas and arguments in a convincing way.

I feel that my strength comes from my blend of creative and academic thinking, along with my ability to talki in fron of people
